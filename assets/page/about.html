Introduction

Founded on 13th July 2023
Ran as a Youth Org in January 2024

Little Lighthouse (LLH) is a youth organization mainly focuses on content creation, which involves sharing knowledge and essential basic skills in life for young minds to be happy in life.

Description

Have you ever wished for a space to freely share your stories, questions, or thoughts without fear of judgment? Dear Anon, powered by Little Lighthouse (LLH), is here for you!

Here's how it works: Whenever you need a safe space to express your thoughts, feelings, or challenges, simply contact us. Our team at LLH will listen with care and reflect on what you share. In return, we will send personalised advice, fresh perspectives, or uplifting words, just like a virtual best friend, through our website or Facebook page.

If your concern is urgent, you can provide your email, and we'll reach out to you as soon as possible with a response.

But Dear Anon is more than just a place to share; it's a community where you are heard, understood, and empowered. Your words can spark change, and we're here to support you every step of the way. So, what's on your mind? Start your journey with Dear Anon today.
